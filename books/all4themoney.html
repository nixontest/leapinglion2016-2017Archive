All 4 The Money

Chris Eyles

  

They keep it small and clean, nothing too big that will draw a lot of attention. They watch crime shows, and understand the basics of what to do and what not to do. They are careful and happy to split the profits. Frank usually brings in the most ideas and targets. He likes to go for drives to scout targets to that he can get out of his house and away from his family because, as he says, they will kill him well before any job-gone-wrong will. Frank and the group operate well together, knowing that each man had a specific role to play in order for them to pull off each job. Usually. When the pressure builds after their biggest heist allegances are tested. Friends that could once be trusted might just be their ultimate downfall.
