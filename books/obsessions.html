Obsessions

Stan Rogal

  

Acclaimed poet and author Stan Rogal’s newest collection of short stories will drag you into a mesmeric, macabre world of fragile characters dealing with crippling obsessions. Befriend a pitiful prostitute; watch a man’s curiosity choke out his life; and be part of a visceral act of voyeurism as you witness passion at its cruelest and darkest forms in the lives of the breaking and the broken.
