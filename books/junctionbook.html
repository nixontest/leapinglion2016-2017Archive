Junction Book

Ken Newton, Ann Ball, William New

  

When the son of the biggest bookmaker in the Junction turns up dead, finding his killer isn't the top priority for the Toronto Police. This leaves Al Green and Associates to take up the case. The trio will venture to the shadiest part of Toronto's Junction, and experience their share of street cars, scandals and slaughterhouses. Their approach might not be the most traditional-or the most ethical-but it's the only way to answer the big question: Who killed Junior O? Follow this trio of hard-bitten detectives as they try to unravel the twisted knot that is the Junction. With history and locations drawn straight from Toronto's past, this is a must-read for mystery fans.
