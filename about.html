About Us

Who are we?

We are Leaping Lion Books; a micro-publishing, independent press residing at York University in Toronto, Canada. Our press is managed by fourth year students in York’s Professional Writing program who are given the opportunity of hands-on experience. These students will put their skills and knowledge to the test within a real life publishing environment. Each year, students are given a manuscript that is selected by the course director, Mike O’Connor and are responsible for transforming that manuscript into a book. They edit, design, produce and market the book––with limited guidance from the course director––while accenting the book with the uniqueness of the Leaping Lions brand. At our press, we pride ourselves in publishing avid Canadian-based authors who are interested in getting their work published. Although we lack years of industry experience, we do make up for that with our youthful enthusiasm, perseverance and curiosity. Our strength lies in the vigorous teamwork of our students who strive for professional success and are always leaping beyond the limits.
